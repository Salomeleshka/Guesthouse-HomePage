Welcome to Wine Loft!

Set in Telavi, within 2.2 km of King Erekle II Palace and 2 km of Giant Plane Tree, Wine Loft • Винный Лофт offers accommodation with a garden and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive. The guest house features mountain views and a terrace. At the guest house, the rooms are fitted with a balcony with a garden view. At Wine Loft • Винный Лофт the rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a shared bathroom. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast. Akhali Shuamta Monastery is 12 km from Wine Loft • Винный Лофт. The nearest airport is Tbilisi International Airport, 84 km from the guest house. We speak your language!
